Meet the Founder

The Man Behind the Jar

Harry Quincy

Founder & CEO

"Every jar is a promise — pure ingredients, crafted with care, delivered with passion."

Harry Quincy's journey started with a simple belief: that everyday food should be extraordinary. Inspired by the rich flavors of nature, he combined the finest roasted peanuts with pure golden honey to create a spread unlike any other.

Today, Harry Quincy's Honey Peanut Butter is more than a product — it's a taste of authenticity, crafted in small batches to preserve flavor, freshness, and quality in every single spoonful.
